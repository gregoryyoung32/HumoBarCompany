Booking & Policies

Transparent by Design

Payment Terms
50% non-refundable deposit secures your date. Remaining balance due 7 days prior to the event. No services rendered without full payment. Your date is not reserved until deposit and signed agreement are received.
Cancellation Policy
Deposit is non-refundable in all cases. 14+ days out: No additional charges. 7–13 days out: 75% of total invoice due. Within 7 days: 100% of total invoice due.
Guest Count Finalization
Final guest count must be submitted 10 days prior to the event. This locks staffing, supplies, and service flow. Increases after this point may result in additional charges. No reductions after finalization.
Alcohol & Responsibility
Client is responsible for all alcohol purchase. We provide clear quantity guidelines to prevent shortages or overspending. Humo Bar Co. does not supply alcohol and is not liable for alcohol-related incidents. We reserve the right to refuse service to intoxicated individuals.

Payment Questions
When is my deposit due?+
Your deposit is due at the time of signing your event agreement. Your date is not held until both the signed contract and the 50% deposit are received by Humo Bar Co.
When is the remaining balance due?+
The remaining 50% balance is due no later than 7 days before your event. Services will not be provided without full payment confirmed in advance.
Is my deposit refundable?+
Deposits are non-refundable. Cancellations 14+ days out incur no additional charges. Cancellations 7–13 days out require 75% of total. Within 7 days requires 100%.
What payment methods are accepted?+
All major credit and debit cards, Apple Pay, and Google Pay. All payments are processed securely through Stripe.
I have a question about my invoice amount.+
If you have questions about your quoted amount or deposit total, please reach out before submitting. Only pay after receiving a formal proposal from Humo Bar Co.

⚡ Connect Stripe to Accept Online Payments
  1. Go to dashboard.stripe.com and create a free account
  2. Complete identity verification (about 5 minutes)
  3. Go to Payment Links → + Create — make one for "Event Deposit" and one for "Remaining Balance"
  4. Open humo-bar-co.html in a text editor and search for YOUR_DEPOSIT_LINK_HERE — replace with your Stripe link
  5. Do the same for YOUR_BALANCE_LINK_HERE
  6. Save the file. Stripe deposits to your bank every 2 business days.
